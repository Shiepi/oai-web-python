Analysis Methods

Random Forest

We use random forest to build a classifer model that uses the video annotations as features to predict the effective score (1-5) that an annotator would give the advertisement. This would tell us whether or not the topic and sentiment of an advertisement is enough to predict how someone would rate its effectiveness.

K-Modes Clustering

We used K-Modes clustering to show how the clustering of strategies and visuals change with sentiment type.
