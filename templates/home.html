Optimizing Ad Impact

Our Purpose

  • Attempt to learn more about what goes into ad optimization and how advertisements are created
  • This is important because of the large media industry and the amount of money that goes into advertising
Essentially, we're trying to provide advertisers guidance, tips and ideas to a successful advertisement design!

Project Objectives

  • Try to predict how a user would rate effectiveness on a 1-5 scale using advertisement topics and sentiments.
  • Analyze how topics and sentiments are grouped based on ad strategies and user-created symbols.
  • Analyze the grouping of topics and sentiments change with respect to sentiment type (positive, neutral, negative).
Goal: To analyze audience reactions and thinking on advertisements and figure out what strongly gets them to react the way we want them to
