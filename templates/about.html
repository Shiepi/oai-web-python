Our Story

As we live in a society that heavily relies on computer technology, the strategy of advertising has also shifted towards more usage of computer technology which advertisers have a technique of spreading information faster and in bigger quantities than ever before.

It has also allowed ease of sharing awareness of your product as using the internet has enabled to connect with everyone all around the world.

Our goal is to analyze & explain how different advertisements manipulate emotions/sentiments to achieve advertising effectiveness through viewers' reactions to what they should do and why they should do what is said in the advertisement.

In simpler terms, OAI is aiming to:

  • Analyze what key emotions/sentiments make an advertisement effective
  • Discover specific patterns of viewer reactions to image/video advertisements
  • Create recommendations for your product depending on what you want in an advertisement
